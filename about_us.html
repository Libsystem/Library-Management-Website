About Us

Our Purpose

Every school has a campus library and every student has access to their school's library, but not every student can make it to the library. Some students commute to class, or live off campus. For these students, we have created a website that will allow each student access to the library database. The purpose of this site is not only for students to be able to view every book in the library, but also check out books and manage their book rentals. Of course, they still have to physically go to the library to pick up and return their books, but they no longer have to worry about going out of their way to see if a book is available or to extend a due date on a book.
